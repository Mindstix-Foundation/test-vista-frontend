Who We Are

Mindstix Foundation Trust is a Pan-India Trust based in Mumbai, Maharashtra. Primarily, we work for the economically constrained populace of the country for the upliftment of the society by digital means. We strive for social transformation and aim to serve the community with the goal of transformation through digital intervention.

We believe that social transformation can be done on the anvil of technological growth and development. We leverage all our activities through the use of existing technology and find ways to upgrade the same with time. We believe that education and technology are two prime fundamental rights that every citizen deserves to have.
